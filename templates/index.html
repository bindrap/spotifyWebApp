Set Up Spotify Credentials
Important: Make sure your Spotify app's Redirect URI is set to:
http://127.0.0.1:5000/callback
Your credentials will be saved locally in a .env file
How to Get Spotify Credentials
  1. Go to Spotify Developer Dashboard:
    Open Dashboard
  2. Log in with your Spotify account
  3. Click "Create App"
    • App Name: "My Playlist Extractor" (or any name)
    • App Description: "Extract my playlists"
    • Redirect URI: http://127.0.0.1:5000/callback ⚠️ This must be exact!
    • Check "Web API" under "Which API/SDKs are you planning to use?"
  4. Click "Save" and then open your new app
  5. Go to "Settings" in your app
  6. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret from the app settings page
Critical: The Redirect URI must be exactly http://127.0.0.1:5000/callback or authentication will fail!
Keep your Client Secret private! Don't share it with anyone.
